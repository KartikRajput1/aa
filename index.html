About Us

We Believe in
“People Power”.
Yours and Ours.

At Lakshyan Foundation, we support athletes in achieving their dreams by offering a nurturing environment and comprehensive support. Our commitment goes beyond performance; we aim to develop well-rounded individuals who excel in sports, contribute to their communities, and inspire future generations. Together, we're shaping a brighter future for sports and making a positive impact on many lives.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a leading institution in nurturing world-class athletes by fostering excellence, inclusivity, and growth. Our goal is to elevate Indian sports to global recognition, inspiring athletes to reach their full potential and positively impact their communities and the nation.

Mission: To provide top-tier coaching, facilities, and holistic support, fostering athletes' skill development and character growth. We promote sportsmanship, discipline, and resilience, empowering athletes from diverse backgrounds to excel and contribute to both the sports community and society.

Objective

To develop top-tier athletes through expert coaching, cutting-edge facilities, and comprehensive support, while promoting sportsmanship, resilience, and community impact.

Proposed Program

KREEDA JAGRITHI

Project ‘Kreeda Jagrithi’ is an opportunity to positively contribute to the lives of underprivileged children in urban areas. Providing children exposure to structured training and enhancing their holistic development.
To promote physical fitness, healthy lifestyle and improve social & cognitive skills among children.
